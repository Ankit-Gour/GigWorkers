About GigConnect

Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between gig workers and local industries in Bundelkhand. By analyzing resumes and recommending skill improvements, we aim to enhance employment opportunities for workers and support businesses with the right talent.

Features of GigConnect

Resume Analysis

We analyze your resume and identify key skills and areas for improvement using AI and NLP technologies.

Personalized Skill Recommendations

Receive tailored upskilling recommendations based on your experience and the demands of local industries.

Job Matching

Get matched with job opportunities that suit your skills and experience, and connect directly with local employers.

Job Alerts

Stay updated with job openings via email or SMS alerts, ensuring you never miss out on opportunities.

How It Works

1. Register

Sign up on the platform and create your profile. Upload your resume and job preferences.

2. Get Skill Insights

Our AI analyzes your skills and suggests relevant training to improve your job prospects.

3. Match with Jobs

We match your profile with local industry job listings, and send you notifications of new opportunities.

4. Get Hired

Apply for jobs that suit your skills and get hired by local businesses in need of your expertise.
