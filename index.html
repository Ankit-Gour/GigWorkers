About GigConnect

At GigConnect, we are dedicated to transforming the way gig workers connect with local industries in Bundelkhand. Our mission is to provide a platform where workers can discover job opportunities, enhance their skills, and create a sustainable career path. Whether you're a seasoned gig worker or just starting, we empower you with tools to succeed in a competitive market.

We bridge the gap between workers and employers by offering:

  • AI-driven resume analysis to identify your strengths.
  • Personalized skill recommendations for upskilling and growth.
  • Local job opportunities matched to your skills and experience.
  • Real-time job alerts to help you stay updated.

Our platform is built to nurture talent, foster growth, and support the gig economy in Bundelkhand.

Features of GigConnect

Resume Analysis

How It Works

1. Register

Sign up on the platform and create your profile. Upload your resume and job preferences.

2. Get Skill Insights

Our AI analyzes your skills and suggests relevant training to improve your job prospects.

3. Match with Jobs

We match your profile with local industry job listings, and send you notifications of new opportunities.

4. Get Hired

Apply for jobs that suit your skills and get hired by local businesses in need of your expertise.
